About Me

I'm a professional video editor with 3+ years of experience in creating engaging visual content that tells stories and captures audiences. My journey started with Alight Motion and has expanded to include various professional tools and techniques.

With expertise in motion graphics, color grading, and visual effects, I transform raw footage into polished, professional videos that meet client objectives and exceed expectations. I've edited 30+ YouTube videos and created numerous motion graphics projects for clients.
